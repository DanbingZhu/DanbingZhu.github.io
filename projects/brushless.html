Brushless Gimbal

Innovation and Practice Program, funded by SJTU. I worked as the team leader of a 3 person team. From 2013 to 2014.

Works

  • Designed the gimbal frame using autoCAD
  • Built the frame using laser cutter
  • Implemented control system based on AlexMos SimpleBGC
  • Conducted aerial photography tests to prove the functionality of the gimbal
